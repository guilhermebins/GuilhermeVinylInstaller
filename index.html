My Experiences

I have 8 years of experience with graphics installations. During this time, I have developed a variety of services, such as huge storefronts, vinyl on cars, trucks and even corporate event stands.

My experience consists of careful, detailed and agile work. Always with the attention and precision that every graphic installation service requires.

I feel good when I finish a job successfully, and that pleases the client. It is great to see the client's smile after a job that turned out beautifully and was completed efficiently.

Check Out Some Of My Services

This was a small truck, where the challenge was to remove the old sticker and glue, and apply the new sticker in just one day. The day was cold, which made removal difficult, but did not prevent the job from being completed on time.

A 3M J30 sticker was applied, which provided a very high-quality finish. And transparent automotive paint was applied to ensure greater longevity for the print.

Even though it is a small job, it is a job to be happy about, due to the quality and completion within the proposed deadline.

Storefronts

This is one of my favorite storefronts. Although it is not the largest I have ever installed, I really like it because of the variety of graphic colors. The biggest challenge was to carefully fit each part of the sticker so that the image looks like just one.

This service involved installing about 40 meters of stickers in approximately 3:30 hours. The client was very satisfied with the agility and quality of the service provided.

Custom Cars

One of my favorite jobs is customizing cars. It's a service that makes me very happy to do. The end result is magical and that's a pleasure.

I have already installed vinyl on several cars, from passenger cars to racing cars. I usually use Oracal vinyl as the material, which guarantees an excellent finish and durability for the client.
